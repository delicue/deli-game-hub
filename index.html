Smash Up

Smash Up is a shufflebuilding game where players combine two factions to dominate bases and score points. I learned how to play this in 2017 with my friends and it was really fun, although I lost more often than you would think.

The trick to this game is picking matching two decks that compliment each other well. For example, if you have one deck that allows you to play extra minions or actions, you need another deck to help you replenish those cards well.
